Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about visiting Vijay Hospital, Narnaul.

Where is Vijay Hospital located in Narnaul?

Vijay Hospital is located directly opposite the Narnaul Bus Stand, at Mamraj Colony, Maharana Pratap Nagar, Narnaul, Haryana 123001 — the moment you step off the bus, we're right there.

Does Vijay Hospital provide 24×7 emergency services?

Yes. We provide round-the-clock 24×7 emergency and trauma care. Call 093067 10615 anytime, day or night.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book by calling 093067 10615 or by filling the short appointment form on this page. We'll call you back to confirm your slot.

What departments are available?

Vijay Hospital offers Cardiology, Orthopedics, General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynaecology, ENT, Surgery, Emergency & Trauma, and Diagnostics/Lab services. PLACEHOLDER — confirm with hospital.
